LeBron's awards

2008 Olympic games

In 2008 LeBron made his debut on USA National Basketball team as a starter and that year USA won the Olympic Gold Medal. The year of 2008, USA National basketball team member has head coach Mike Krzyzewski, in forward positioners: Carlos Boozer, Lebron James, Tayshuan Prince, Carmelo Anthony, guard positioners: Jason Kidd, Deron Williams, Micheal Redd, Dwyane Wade, Chris Paul, center postioners: Dwight Howard and Chris Bosch, captain of team is the legend, Kobe Bryant.

2012 Olympic games

LeBron has played 2 time in USA National Basketball team and in 2012 Olympic games they won again Gold medal at the Olympic games. That year Kobe Bryant played his last Olympic Games.

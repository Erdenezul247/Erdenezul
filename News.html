LeBron born in December 30, 1984. He is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Assiciation. Nicknamed "King James". He has competed in ten NBA Finals with eight consecutive appearances from 2011-2018, winning four championships, and he also won the inaugural NBA Cup in 2023 with the Lakers, and two Olypic gold medals as a member of USA men's national team./p>

James grew up playing basketball for St. Vincent-St. Mary High School in his hometown of Akron, Ohio. He was heavily touted by the national media as a future NBA superstar for his all-around, passing athleticism and playmaking abilities.
